    What is iTrust?
    iTrust is a network reference tool to link professionals who have worked together and are in a position to vouch for the professional experience of each other. This improves the credibility of the profiles on the network.

    How can I use iTrust?
    Trusken enables you to utilise your coins to enhance the profile of your trusted connections. By trusting your connections, you will get become part of large trust network. For each person you trust, you will have to spent 500 Trusken coins but they earn 2000 Trusken coins.

    Why do I need to spend Trusken Coins to iTrust People?
    A robust system requires that its networks consist of real relationships. We are more inclined to spend for people who we genuinely believe in. In the long term, given that the person whom you iTrust will get to earn 2000 coins, the system rewards genuine trust relationships.

    Why can I not see any people to iTrust currently?
    When the network gathers size, you will be able to see all colleagues who have worked in the same organisation during your tenure there. The reason why you probably cannot see anyone to iTrust currently is because none of the colleagues have yet enrolled and received their Trusken verification.

    Why can I not iTrust someone who has not worked in the same organisation during my tenure?
    Trusken is creating a Trust Platform. Trusken iTrust ensures that the trust relationship is genuine and not frivolous like on many of the existing social media platforms. Hence you can only trust colleagues who have worked with you in the same organisation during your tenure there. Also, both of you should have verified work experience.

    I and my colleague have worked together and want to iTrust him. However, his work experience is not verified by Trusken. How can I iTrust him?
    A Trusken connection means you can always trust. We would request him to get his work experience verified through Trusken. Once the verification is complete, you will be able to iTrust him.

    I can vouch my friend’s professional competence as I regularly seek his advice on technical issues. Why can I not iTrust him?
    A credible trust network requires certain checks and balances. These may not seem fair but till a better solution is found, these would be required. If you have any suggestions, feel free to share them with is at support@trusken.com.
