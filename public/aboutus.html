About Trusken

Blockchain Technology has opened numerous opportunities for improving efficiency through transparency across sectors. Trusken is a platform where counterparties can quickly establish trust using the power of Blockchain.
Since the days mankind has required to exchange goods and services, the establishment of trust has been essential for trade. To establish this trust, numerous intermediaries like Banks, Merchants and myriad other middlemen have been required. The robust Blockchain technology which has powered the creation of crypto currencies like Bitcoin, Ethereum etc., can also be used to create a Trust network.
Let us explain with an example. When you swipe your card to purchase something, the banking system takes a hefty commission, this is because they bring trust into the system. This issue of trust can easily be solved through blockchain technologies. Now the same analogy can be used for things as diverse as a cab ride to background verification. A cab hailing company’s primary function is to establish trust that the ride will be smooth. The same functionality can be replicated by a blockchain trust network by establishing the bona fides / history of the driver and the rider. Every time two parties transact, the same can be recorded on a blockchain.
The Trusken platform will allow the community to quickly establish trust between counterparties. Initially Trusken is using the platform to create Trust between Employers and Professionals. Blockchain offers numerous solutions for a multitude of use cases which Trusken will enable; as the community grows.
The challenge for the success of such a trust network is scale. To rapidly build users, Trusken is liberally issuing coins to early adopters. These coins will enable the community to create value and avail benefits. As millions join, the Trusken Coins becomes more valuable and reach their highest potential.

By joining, you contribute towards the establishment of a Trust Network that removes the multitude of middlemen in a diverse range of businesses. These commissions add up to a lot.   • Why do I need to provide my public linkedin employment profile? Why does Trusken verify it?

  • Is this a Ponzi, pyramid or Multi LevelMarketing scheme?
    Ponzi scams take money from new members and give it to earlier members. Trusken is completely free. There is no money to be collected or distributed. Trusken does reward its members with Trusken Coins. More coins are given to early members and to those who help build the community. The value of these coins can increase as the number of users and frequency of use increases.
  • Can I register multiple times and get more Trusken Coins?

Providing false details, impersonation, or opening more than one account will result in the termination of all accounts and loss of rewards earned.
